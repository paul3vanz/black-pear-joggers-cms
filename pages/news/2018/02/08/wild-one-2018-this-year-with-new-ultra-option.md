Woohoo, we've done it.

We have![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/091916_1039_2016SportsA12.png) agreed all the necessary information for our fun, multi lap marathon and can get advertising to the masses to try and incre![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/091916_1039_2016SportsA11.png)ase numbers and our much needed and appreciated contributions to our nominated charities, Worcestershire Young Carers (YSS) and Maggs Day Centre).

We have a date - **Sunday, 22nd July 2018![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/save-the-date-with-laurel-leaves-01--795x562.jpg)**

We have a venue - **Old Elizabethans Cricket Club, Droitwich Road, Worcester, WR3 7SN** (same as last year) ![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

We know the course - 10 laps of off-road fun around a combination of grass, wooded trails and tarmac (2.62 miles per lap)![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

We have several event options:![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

- The Wild Mile
- Marathon **solo** - the full 26.2 on your own
- Marathon as a double act - in **pairs**... 26.2 between you
- Marathon as a team - **teams of five​** as a relay event![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/IMG_4071.png)

And we even have a **NEW** event this year![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

- The Wild **Ultra** - 26.3 miles and above, so anything above 10 laps and in fact as many laps as you can do in 7 hours

What do you get?![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

- Bespoke medal and goody bag for all finishers. (Who can upstage last year's design???)![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/Wild-one-medal.png)

Prizes will be awarded for:![](https://bpj.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/green-tick.jpg)

- Fastest marathon times
- Best fancy dress
- Fastest lap
- Furthest Ultra.... to list but a few

Entry is £16 ​per person for affiliated runners (£18 ​unaffiliated). (There are no booking fees/charges for entering online.)

Wild Mile​ kid’s race entry is £3.50 (enter online or on the day).

**SO...**

get you diaries out, put the date in, form your teams (or fly solo), enter through [Entry Central](https://www.entrycentral.com/wild-one) and watch this space for further announcements about the all important fancy dress theme decision.
